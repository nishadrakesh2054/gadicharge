About Us

We Provide Best Car Charging Experiences.

Gadi Charge Pvt. Ltd. (GadiCharge) is a Charging as a Service (CaaS) provider offering end-to-end Electric Vehicle (EV) charging infrastructure and solutions, creating an accessible, reliable, and efficient network of charge points that cater to the needs of EV users in Nepal, while contributing towards achieving the nation’s EV adoption goals. GadiCharge builds and operates own charging stations as well as onboards and supports franchisees (aspiring entrepreneurs, existing corporates working in a varied range of industries) to establish and operate EV Charging Stations under our brand.

Our commitment to innovation and reliability has made GadiCharge a trusted partner for franchisees, as we deliver unparalleled support in the electric vehicle sector.

Faq’s

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How does the EV charging process work?

To charge your EV, simply plug it into an available charging station. Depending on the charger type, it may take anywhere from 30 minutes (fast charging) to several hours (slow charging). The charging process stops automatically once your battery is full.

2. How do I find the nearest charging station?

You can find nearby charging stations using our website, mobile app, or third-party platforms like Google Maps and PlugShare. Simply enter your location, and the system will show the closest available chargers.

3. Can I charge multiple vehicles simultaneously?

Some charging stations have multiple connectors, allowing multiple vehicles to charge at the same time. However, the total power may be distributed, which could affect charging speed.

4. Are there different charging speeds available?

Yes, EV chargers come in different types: Level 1 (slow, using a standard home outlet), Level 2 (faster, commonly found at public stations), and DC fast chargers (rapid charging, typically for highways and commercial use).

5. What payment methods are accepted?

Our charging stations accept multiple payment methods, including credit/debit cards, mobile wallets, RFID cards, and subscriptions via our app.

6. What types of connectors are supported?

Our stations support popular EV connectors, including Type 2, CCS, CHAdeMO, and Tesla Superchargers. Make sure to check compatibility with your vehicle before charging.
